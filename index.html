FOREX TRADING

What is forex trading? Forex trading is the simultaneous buying and selling of the world’s currencies on a decentralised global market.
It’s also referred to as foreign exchange or FX market. As one of the largest and most liquid financial markets in the world,
its total average turnover per day is reported to exceed $5 trillion. Forex is not based on central places or exchanges.
So it is open for trading 24 hours in a day, from Saturday night to Friday night.

INTEGRATING ETHEREUM & OTHERS TO CREATIVE MORE VALUES

By integrating various ecosystems (Ethereum and others) we enhance the liquidity and value of original assets on Onyx. Rather than building an ecosystem that competes with Ethereum, we aim to leverage on its strength such as providing asset aggregation contracts, connecting to Ethereum smart contracts.

MULTI-CHAIN & CROSS-CHAIN

$Onyx token supports cross-chains internally (between Forex and other $Onyx chains) and among other
chains (Ethereum, etc). Through decentralized validators can safely move assets between
Forex and other chains. This comes with high transaction speed, low transaction cost,
ability to trade assets, and so on useful cases.
